Technical Analysis

In finance, technical analysis is an analysis methodology for forecasting the direction of prices through the study of past market data and analyzing statistical trends from trading activity, primarily price and volume, to identify trading opportunities to enter (buy) and exit (sell) trades. It is ideal for quick decisions and short term portfolio growth.
