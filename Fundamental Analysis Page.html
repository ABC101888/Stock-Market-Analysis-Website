Fundamental Analysis

In finance, fundamental analysis attempts to measure a security's (stock's) intrinsic value (or theoretical value) by examining related economic and financial factors including the balance sheet, strategic initiatives, microeconomic indicators, and consumer behavior, which influence its price in the future. It is ideal for long term portfolio growth.
