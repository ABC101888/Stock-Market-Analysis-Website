<h2>Purpose</h2>
<div>
<p><b><i>Not many individuals are knowledgeable about the stock market, this website aims to educate
individuals to understand the market and investment terminology as well as offer helpful tools to help
guide their investments.</i></b>
</p>
</div>
</div>
<div class="card">
<h2>About</h2>
<div>
<p> <b>Most new investors are aged 18 to 30+ years old</b> because investors must be 18 years old to buy stocks and most new investors have
a stable income after graduating high schol or university. Most young investors do not understand the market well.
<b>The idea is to educate young investors to understand the market and key indicators to evaluate a stock to determine its intrinsic value (or
theoretical value).</b>
<br><br>
As investors, community information (i.e., stock data & analysis) is what enables investors to participate fully within the
investing community and stock market <b>to grow their portfolio.</b><b>The website aggregates links to other helpful resources instead of googling for
relevant information. It creates a SparkNotes verison of the stock market and makes associated terminology understandable to new investors.</b>
<br><br>
Therefore, the website streamlines the information and visualization, so users do not feel overwhelmed or get overload with information.
It educates investors on key indicators to evaluate a stock to determine its intrinsic value (or theoretical value). It offers helpful tools that
will do an analysis on the stocks for the users base on the stock’s ticker symbol (i.e., Apple’s ticker symbol is AAPL). Information will be provided
to explain these analyses to the users.
<br>
</p>
